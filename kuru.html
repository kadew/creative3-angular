This is a comic I've been working on slowly over a couple of years. It follows the story of a playthrough of Pokemon Red Version I did a while back, under "Nuzlocke" rules.

Nuzlocke rules are as follows:

  1. Only capture the first pokemon encountered on a given route
  2. If a pokemon of yours faints in battle, it is considered dead and must be released.

And that's how far I've drawn :V

I made another site for the comic a while back, but I wanted to test this with Angular as a means of displaying every page in once long continuous scroll, since Angular's ng-repeat method is good for going through lists like that quickly. And so, I made this for the creative project.

Fun fact: the title, "Kurukkoo!" is the sound pidgey make in the game.
